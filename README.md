# FNP Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project is an interactive FNP (Ferns N Petals) Sales Analysis Dashboard developed using Microsoft Excel.
The dashboard provides detailed insights into sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, delivery analysis, product performance,
and occasion-based revenue trends.

The main objective of this project is to help businesses monitor sales activities, analyze customer spending patterns, identify top-performing products,
and support data-driven business decisions using interactive dashboard visualizations.

--------------------------------------------------

## Business Problem

E-commerce and gifting businesses generate large amounts of sales and customer data daily. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to:

* Track overall sales performance  
* Identify high-revenue occasions  
* Analyze customer spending behavior  
* Monitor delivery performance  
* Identify top-performing products  
* Track monthly and hourly sales trends  
* Analyze city-wise order distribution  
* Improve business and marketing strategies  

This dashboard solves these challenges through interactive reporting and data visualization techniques in Microsoft Excel.

--------------------------------------------------

## KPI Metrics Used

* Total Orders — Total number of customer orders  
* Total Revenue — Overall sales revenue generated  
* Average Customer Spent — Average spending per customer  
* Order-Delivery Time — Average delivery duration  
* Revenue by Occasion — Sales generated during occasions/festivals  
* Product Revenue — Revenue contribution by products/categories  

--------------------------------------------------

## Key Insights

* The dashboard recorded 1000 total customer orders.  
* Total revenue exceeded ₹6.7 Lakhs.  
* Average customer spending remained around ₹3,290.  
* Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan occasions generated the highest revenue.  
* Sweets and Raksha Bandhan categories contributed major sales revenue.  
* Evening hours showed higher customer ordering activity.  
* Monthly revenue peaked during September and November.  
* Certain products consistently outperformed others in revenue generation.  
* Major cities contributed significantly to overall order volume.
